Program Information

Mission Statement

As the owner and operator of Spatulini's Home Childcare, my mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for young children to learn and grow. I am dedicated to supporting the physical, emotional, and cognitive development of each child in my care, and to fostering a sense of community and belonging among our families. I strive to create a home away from home where children feel loved and supported, and where they can develop the skills and confidence they need to thrive.

Child Safety Details

At my home daycare, child safety is a top priority. I take several steps to ensure that the children in my care are kept safe and secure at all times. This includes regularly checking the space for potential hazards and taking steps to prevent accidents. I also have safety gates and other barriers in place to prevent children from accessing unsafe areas. In addition, I have an emergency plan in place in case of any unforeseen events. As a provider, I am committed to creating a safe and nurturing environment for the children in my care. I believe that by taking these steps, I can help give parents peace of mind while their children are in my care.

About Me

Donna Spatulini

Welcome to my home daycare! My name is Donna Spatulini and I have been providing high-quality child care services in my home for the past 15 years. I have a passion for helping children learn and grow in a safe and nurturing environment. I understand that choosing the right care for your child is an important decision, and I am dedicated to providing a warm and loving home away from home for your little ones. My daycare offers a structured daily routine with plenty of opportunities for play, learning, and exploration. I believe in open communication and welcome feedback from parents to ensure the best possible experience for your child. Thank you for considering my home daycare for your child's care and early education needs.
